About This Project

This corporate brand video was created for TechInnovate's product launch event. The client wanted a sleek, modern video that would showcase their new AI-powered analytics platform while maintaining their established brand identity.

The video combines live-action footage of their team and workspace with dynamic motion graphics that visualize the platform's capabilities. I worked closely with their marketing team to ensure the messaging was clear and impactful, while crafting visuals that would engage both technical and non-technical audiences.
